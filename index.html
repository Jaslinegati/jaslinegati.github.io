About me

Analyst by training,
storyteller by practice

I'm a Data Analyst and Data Scientist who believes data only matters when it drives decisions. My work spans the full analytics stack — from cleaning messy raw data and writing SQL queries to building machine learning models and creating dashboards that non-technical stakeholders can actually use.

My focus is on customer behaviour, retention, and predictive analytics — the questions that sit at the intersection of data science and business strategy. I'm drawn to projects where the insight leads to a clear action, not just a chart.

When I'm not building models or wrangling data, I'm exploring new datasets, sharpening my SQL, and thinking about how AI can automate the repetitive parts of analysis so analysts can spend more time on the interesting parts.
